Terminal app written in Ruby that charts a scatterplot directly into the terminal window using the Curses gem. Reads and parses CSV data to identify and collect date and temperature pairs, then draws those pairs directly within the console. Everything on screen is coded, included x and y-axis, title and keys which adjust automatically as the data scales.

Written in two days together with another Coder Academy student for our first Ruby project.
